1.Technology

The Greek terms techne and logos, when transliterated, are the origin of the word technology. Techne is the Greek word for art, skill, craft, as well as the method or process by which something is acquired. The Greek term logos translates as "word," "utterance," "saying," or "expression". Today's technology has fundamentally altered the way we live, work, and communicate.A few instances of how technology has grown significantly and provided our society options never before possible include smartphones, social media, artificial intelligence, and virtual reality.
